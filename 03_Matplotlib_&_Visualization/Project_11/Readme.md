### TCS Stock Price Analysis

- Dataset: TCS.NS live data Vai yfinance (2024-2026)
- Tools: python, pandas, matplotlib, yfinance, os, jupyter notebook.
- Concepts Covered :    a) sorted column according to dates.
                        b) plot open,high,low,close of stock price on date range of 200 days.
                        c) calculated the moving average (MA20,MA50) and Ploted the same on chart.
                        d) Annotation, Dual subplot & Chart Export.
- Outcome: Visualized TCS Stock Price Trends With MA20,MA50 indicators & Volume Analysis.
